Our Heritage

From ashes to triumph

From ashes to triumph, Barcode evolved from a coffee shop born of personal tragedy into a culinary empire. Mughul Hoque transformed loss into legacy, blending modern cafe culture with authentic local flavors like mezban and fuchka, now deeply rooted in Chittagong's evolving food heritage.

Our Story
